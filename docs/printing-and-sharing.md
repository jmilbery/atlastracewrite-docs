# Printing & Sharing

After generating a worksheet, you can preview it on screen and then print, share, or save it as a PDF.

## PDF Preview

The right side of the screen shows a full preview of your generated worksheet. You can scroll through the pages if your text spans multiple pages.

## Toolbar Actions

Three buttons in the top-right toolbar let you work with your worksheet:

### Share

Tap the **share button** (square with arrow) to open the standard iPad share sheet. From here you can:

- Send the PDF via AirDrop, Messages, or Mail
- Save to Files, iCloud Drive, or other cloud services
- Copy or markup the PDF

### Save

Tap the **save button** (arrow into document) to save the worksheet as a PDF file. A file picker appears where you can choose the save location and filename. The default filename is `AtlasTraceWrite_Worksheet.pdf`.

### Print

Tap the **print button** (printer icon) to send the worksheet directly to an AirPrint-compatible printer. The standard iPad print dialog appears where you can select your printer, number of copies, and page range.

## Tips for Best Results

- **Use US Letter paper** (8.5" x 11") for correct sizing — the worksheets are designed for this paper size.
- **Print at 100% scale** — avoid "fit to page" options that might shrink the ruled lines.
- **Use a laser printer** if available — the light gray traceable letters print more consistently on laser printers than inkjet.
- **Check your ink levels** — very low ink can make the already-light traceable letters invisible.

## Going Back to Edit

Tap the **Start Over** button (circular arrow) in the top-left toolbar to return to the Speak step. Your text is preserved — you can make edits and tap **Generate Worksheet** again without re-entering everything. Use the **X button** in the Speak step to clear text and start a completely new worksheet.
