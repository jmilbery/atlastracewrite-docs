# Practice Templates

The **orange templates button** provides ready-made text for common handwriting exercises. Templates are available in both the Speak and keyboard input modes.

## Alphabet

Three alphabet options:

- **A a B b C c … Z z** — Paired uppercase and lowercase letters
- **A–Z Uppercase Only** — All 26 capital letters
- **a–z Lowercase Only** — All 26 lowercase letters

## Sight Words

[Dolch sight words](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dolch_word_list) matched to your current grade level setting. Three options:

- **All Sight Words** — The complete word list for the selected grade
- **10 Random Words** — A random selection of 10 words (different each time)
- **20 Random Words** — A random selection of 20 words (different each time)

The sight word lists are based on the Dolch 220 service words and 95 common nouns, organized by grade:

| Grade | Word Source |
|-------|-----------|
| Grade 1 | Pre-Primer + Grade 1 Dolch words |
| Grade 2 | Grade 2 Dolch words |
| Grade 3 | Grade 3 Dolch words |
| Grade 4 | Dolch 95 Common Nouns |

## Word Families

Eight common word families for phonics practice:

| Family | Example Words |
|--------|--------------|
| -at | cat, bat, hat, mat, sat, rat, fat, pat |
| -an | can, fan, man, ran, van, pan, tan |
| -ig | big, dig, fig, pig, wig, jig |
| -og | dog, fog, hog, log, jog, bog |
| -op | hop, mop, top, pop, stop, drop |
| -ug | bug, hug, mug, rug, tug, dug, jug |
| -et | get, let, met, net, pet, set, wet, yet |
| -en | den, hen, men, pen, ten, then, when |

Each family is a separate menu item — tap one to generate a worksheet with just that family's words.

## Practice Sentences

Grade-appropriate sentences for connected handwriting practice. The sentences use vocabulary appropriate for the selected grade level.

The **Pangram** option generates the classic sentence that contains every letter of the alphabet — useful for practicing all 26 letters in context.
